# XRegExp

[[src](https://github.com/slevithan/xregexp)]

XRegExp by Steven Levithan extends ECMAScript 5 `RegExp` with additional
features.

Succeeded by [Regex+](regexplus.md) by the same author.

## Users

- [Shumway](shumway.md) used XRegExp in its first implementation of ActionScript
  3 `RegExp`.
